## Founding criteria (authoritative — spec §17.6 / Appendix C)

WO-87a seeds these 11 criterion names; weights, per-band scoring descriptors, and industry variants are **admin-managed** and owner-set in WO-87b (no spec-mandated values).

1. Type of business
2. Location
3. Means of doing business
4. Discuss the industry
5. What sets the business apart from its competitors
6. Describe unique success factors
7. Mission and Vision statement
8. Intellectual property
9. Goals and objectives
10. Culture
11. Legal Environment — licences, permits, health/environmental regulations, industry-specific regulations, zoning/building requirements, trademarks, copyrights, patents purchased/pending, insurance coverage

Overall-grade bands (spec §17.5): Exceptional 90%+, Strong 75–89%, Developing 60–74%, Needs Work below 60%.
